About Us

Building trust through accurate, reliable, and professional valuation services.

Who We Are

Sec & M Co. Ltd is a leading Kenyan-based assets valuation firm. We are fully accredited by the Institution of Surveyors of Kenya (ISK) and licensed by the Valuers Registration Board (VRB) and Estate Agents Registration Board (EARB).

With over 10 years of active presence in the Real Estate Industry, we have offices in Nairobi, Mombasa, and Nakuru. Our firm is 100% locally owned and brings together a group of experts with vast experience in property valuations, feasibility studies, and management consultancy.
