Sec & M Co. Ltd is a Kenyan-based assets valuation firm with an outstanding depth of knowledge in valuation services, having over 20 years active presence in the Real Estate Industry with registered offices in Nairobi, Kenya. The firm has brought together a group of experts who have gained vast experience in complex assets valuations, feasibility studies and management consultancy services in Kenya. The firm is 100% locally owned.

With a breadth of experience advising government, businesses and institutions across a wide variety of industry segments, Sec & M Co. Limited is ideally placed to solve your complex valuation needs. Sec & M Co. Limited reputation is built on experience, integrity, thoroughness and our ability to meet each project on time and on budget.

Headquartered in Nairobi with strategic regional offices in Mombasa and Nakuru, we serve a diverse portfolio of clients, from major financial institutions and public sector entities to international NGOs and private investors.

Our team of highly qualified professionals is regulated by the Royal Institution of Chartered Surveyors (RICS) and the Institution of Surveyors of Kenya (ISK), ensuring every engagement adheres to the highest global standards.

In accordance to the provision of the Valuers Act (Cap 532) and Estate Agents Act (Cap 533) Laws of Kenya, we have a professional Indemnity cover from Pacis Insurance Co. Ltd. of One Billion Kenya Shillings.
