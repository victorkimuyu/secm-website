Educational Institutions

SECM Valuers partners with leading universities and colleges to value their extensive property portfolios. We have worked with Kenyatta University, Maseno University, Methodist University, and Bondo University to ensure their assets are accurately recorded and insured.

Government & Parastatals

We are a trusted partner for government bodies and parastatals, handling complex valuations for infrastructure, utility assets, and national resources. Our portfolio includes National Water, Kenya Airports Authority, Kenya Railways, and numerous County Governments.
